Mako is an open-source SQL client where the AI is the interface. Connect your databases, ask questions in plain English, and get real queries executed against real data -- no copy-pasting schemas into ChatGPT.

## What You Get

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="One Console, Every Database" icon="random">
    PostgreSQL, MongoDB, BigQuery, MySQL, ClickHouse, Redshift, SQLite,
    Snowflake, and Cloudflare D1. Switch databases without switching tools.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI That Sees Your Schema" icon="magnifier">
    The agent inspects your actual tables and columns, writes queries, executes
    them, reads results, and iterates. No hallucinated column names.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Console-First" icon="rocket">
    Queries land in a real editor with syntax highlighting, auto-complete, and
    multi-cursor. The console is the product, not the chat.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gets Smarter Over Time" icon="document">
    A self-directive system lets the agent learn your schema conventions, naming
    patterns, and common queries. It improves the more you use it.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Who Is It For?

- **Founders & CEOs** -- Ask business questions, get SQL answers, no analyst required
- **Product Managers** -- Direct access to product metrics without waiting on eng
- **Engineers** -- A modern SQL client with AI superpowers, open source, self-hostable
- **Data Analysts** -- Let AI handle the boilerplate while you focus on the analysis
