Mako is an open-source, AI-powered data platform. Connect your existing databases and third-party sources, sync them into a unified warehouse, and use AI to write queries and analyze your data — all from one interface.

## What Mako Does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Connect Everything" icon="random">
    Hook up PostgreSQL, MongoDB, BigQuery, Stripe, Close CRM, PostHog, and more. Mako talks to all of them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sync & Unify" icon="rocket">
    Pull data from scattered sources into one warehouse with resumable, chunked ETL pipelines.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ask Questions" icon="magnifier">
    Talk to your data in plain English. Mako's AI agent inspects schemas, writes SQL/MongoDB queries, and explains results.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collaborate" icon="document">
    Save queries as consoles, share them with your team, and expose them via API.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Who Is It For?

- **Founders & CEOs** — get answers to business questions without waiting for a data analyst
- **Data Analysts** — automate ETL and let AI write the boring SQL
- **Product Managers** — make data-driven decisions with direct access to product metrics
- **Engineers** — a modern SQL client with AI superpowers, open source, self-hostable
